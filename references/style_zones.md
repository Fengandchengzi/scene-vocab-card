# Style Zone 速查表

直接复制对应风格的 prompt 文本，替换 scene_image_prompt 中的【风格区】。

---

## 1. default — 现代教育插画

```
modern educational illustration style, clean vector-based art with soft hand-drawn texture, gentle rounded shapes, warm and friendly color palette (muted terracotta, sage green, cream white, warm ochre), soft even lighting (no harsh shadows), subtle grain texture overlay, 4:3 ratio, no photorealistic elements, no dark or dramatic lighting, no cinematic atmosphere
```

---

## 2. watercolor — 柔和水彩

```
soft watercolor illustration style, gentle transparent washes with visible paper texture, delicate ink outlines, pastel and muted tones (dusty rose, soft teal, warm cream, light lavender), natural color bleeding at edges, white paper showing through in highlights, hand-painted feel with slight imperfections, 4:3 ratio
```

---

## 3. hand_painted_anime — 手绘动画风

```
Japanese hand-painted animation art style, rich atmospheric backgrounds with meticulous detail, soft cel-shading with warm color grading, lush natural elements (grass, clouds, sunlight), gentle and nostalgic mood, painterly sky with layered clouds, characters with simple expressive features, 4:3 ratio
```

---

## 4. line_drawing — 黑白线描

```
black and white line drawing illustration, clean precise ink lines with varying thickness, minimal crosshatching for shading, generous white space, no color fill (pure linework), architectural precision for objects, loose gestural lines for figures, subtle stippling for texture, 4:3 ratio
```

---

## 5. pixel_art — 像素画

```
16-bit pixel art style, limited color palette (max 32 colors), crisp pixel edges with no anti-aliasing, retro game aesthetic, isometric or side-view perspective, chunky readable shapes, dithering for gradients, nostalgic warm tones, 4:3 ratio
```

---

## 6. chinese_ink — 中国水墨

```
traditional Chinese ink wash painting style (shui-mo), xuan paper texture with natural ink diffusion, varying ink density from deep black to pale grey washes, minimal color accents (vermillion seal, pale mineral pigments), generous negative space (liu-bai), calligraphic brushwork for lines, contemplative and elegant atmosphere, 4:3 ratio
```

---

## 7. realistic — 写实油画

```
painterly realistic illustration style, visible brushstrokes with oil painting texture, rich saturated color palette, naturalistic lighting with soft ambient occlusion, classical composition with balanced focal points, detailed material rendering (fabric folds, metal sheen, wood grain), warm undertones in shadows, 4:3 ratio
```
